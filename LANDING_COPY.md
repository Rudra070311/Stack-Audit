# FAQ

## 1. Does StackAudit require login or billing information?

No. You can run a complete audit without creating an account or entering payment details. We only ask for email after the report is generated if you want to save or share it.

---

## 2. How accurate are the savings recommendations?

The recommendations are based on current public pricing, team size, usage patterns, and tool overlap logic. We intentionally use rule-based calculations instead of AI-generated math for reliability.

---

## 3. Does this replace finance tooling?

No. StackAudit is designed as a lightweight AI spend analysis tool for founders, engineering managers, and startups trying to understand tooling inefficiencies quickly.

---

## 4. Why do you recommend alternative tools sometimes?

Many teams accidentally pay for overlapping capabilities across multiple AI products. The audit highlights where a cheaper plan or alternative could provide similar value for your specific use case.

---

## 5. Is my company data shared publicly?

No. Public share links exclude identifying information such as company name and email address. Only anonymized tooling and savings information is shown.
